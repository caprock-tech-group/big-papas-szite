Our Story

A Texas family building something worth coming hungry for.

Big Papa's Texas Loaded Potatoes was born from a simple idea: take a humble baked potato and turn it into a meal people remember. We're based in Claude, Texas, and built around generous portions, quality ingredients, and genuine hospitality.

Our mission is simple: serve loaded potatoes that feel premium, satisfying, and unmistakably Texas.
